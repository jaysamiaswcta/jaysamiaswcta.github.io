Illustration of the Shining Twins
2014




Yin.
Yang.
Teaching you to be
productive in every endeavor,
through which you should learn
Circa forever.

Although the lesson may not be realized immediately,
commemorate everything you have done,
for your actions shaped you into the person you are today.

Yin and yang come with simple rules:
One.
Love.
Love everything.
Appreciate every aspect of everything.
Like… everything.
An open mind calls for a well rounded attitude.

Two.
Marinate in a culture not native to you.
Let the foreign words hit you like boulders,
waking you up,
opening your eyes to this
tubular planet and it’s diversity.
